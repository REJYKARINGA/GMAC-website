About Us

GMAC has been a trusted name in the travel industry for more than 38 years. We have a team that we can honestly say are the best travel agents in Greece, fluent in many languages with deep knowledge of the country that comes with a love for it. Our offices are conveniently located just a block from Syntagma Square in downtown Athens. Established in 1983, GMAC continues to be a widely acknowledged and reliable tour operator.
Having acquired a good reputation and good reviews from its travellers and its co-operators. Our dedicated and professional multilingual personnel, will be ready to serve you from the first "Welcome" to the last "Goodbye".
